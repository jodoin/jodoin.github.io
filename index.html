Research Areas

My work at the VITALab (Video & Images Theory and Analytics Laboratory) focuses on advancing foundational deep learning approaches for reliable and reproducible biomedical feature extraction, providing the essential AI infrastructure required for interpretable diagnostic and prognostic models in neuroimaging and cardiology.

Research areas :

  • Deep learning
  • MRI brain imaging
  • MRI and Ultrasound cardiac imaging
  • Diffusion MRI Harmonization
  • Multimodal Clinical AI
  • Medical Generative Models
  • Uncertainty Quantification & Robustness in Medical Imaging
  • Large-Scale Medical Imaging Datasets & Benchmarking
  • White-Matter Tractography & Reinforcement Learning
